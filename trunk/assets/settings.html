Settings


Use HTTPS
Uncheck if you have login problems or do not need a secure connection.
Log out on hide
Logs you out of Pardus whenever the app is in the background.
Space chart display size
Sets the amount of tiles displayed in the space chart (no effect without Premium account).
Partial page refreshes
Only refreshes sections of the Nav screen that have changed. Prerequisite for ship animation.
Ship animation
Shows your ship's movement when changing location. "Partial page refreshes" required.
Ship rotation
Makes ships face the direction they are heading.
Clear cache
Deletes all cookies and cached websites.
